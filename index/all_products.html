Geometris Products

The Geometris whereQube is an in-vehicle installed telematics unit that is designed to provide a wide range of in-vehicle functions. The whereQube supports a variety of reporting intervals and power conservation profiles that are simple to use. Vehicle journey reports based on time, distance, and direction of travel can be easily created from the information reported by the whereQube. Additionally, all vehicle starts and stops, loss of GPS, low battery, and input state changes can be reported.

Introduction to Tool Tracking

Using the IP67 rated tool tags allows companies to monitor tool location and ensuring efficient tool management and reducing the risk of loss or theft.

  • Enhanced tool management and accountability
  • QR Code to allow Seamless Tool Registration
  • Battery Charge Level Reported
  • Compatible with all BLE-capable Geometris devices (87, 88, 96, 79 Series)

RP 1226 is recommended practice to be used for ELD/Telematics in newer models 2019+ and above, you will still be able to get data from the J1939 Diagnostic connector. To confirm the RP 1226 port on the vehicle, request that the customer search for RP 1226 or Third-Party Communication in the manual. Alternatively, you may advise them to contact the manufacturer and inquire about the location of the RP1226 port as well as reference the Body Builder Manuals.
